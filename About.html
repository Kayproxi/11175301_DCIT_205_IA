Welcome to the Computer Science Department of the University of Ghana, the leading institution for computer science education and research in Ghana. Our department was established in 1974 as the first computer science department in sub-Saharan Africa. Since then, we have been at the forefront of advancing the field of computing in Ghana and beyond. Our department offers undergraduate and graduate programs in computer science, as well as diploma and certificate courses in various areas of computing. Our curriculum is designed to equip our students with the theoretical and practical skills they need to excel in the rapidly changing world of technology. Our faculty members are experts in their fields, with research interests ranging from artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, software engineering, human-computer interaction, and more. Our students and alumni have gone on to pursue successful careers in academia, industry, government, and entrepreneurship. Our department is also actively engaged in outreach and collaboration with other institutions, organizations, and communities. We host regular seminars, workshops, hackathons, and competitions to promote computer science education and innovation. We also partner with local and international stakeholders to address societal challenges and opportunities using computing solutions. Some of our recent projects include developing a mobile app for COVID-19 contact tracing, creating a chatbot for online learning, and designing a smart irrigation system for farmers. We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our department, our programs, our faculty, our research, and our activities. If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to contact us. We look forward to hearing from you and welcoming you to our computer science community. Thank you for your interest in our department.
