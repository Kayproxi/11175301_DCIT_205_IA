The department of computer science offers an eight week intensive course. Applicants will be given certificates upon completion.

Opportunities

There are many opportunities for computer science students to learn new skills, gain experience, and earn money. Some of the opportunities are:

Freelance web developer: You can create websites for clients using your knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web technologies. You can set your own hours and rates, and work on projects that interest you

Data entry clerk: You can enter data into databases, spreadsheets, or other software systems. You can work from home or in an office, and choose the amount of work you want to do.

Computer repair technician: You can fix hardware and software problems for customers, such as installing new components, updating drivers, removing viruses, and troubleshooting errors. You can work independently or for a company, and charge by the hour or by the service

Video game designer: You can design and develop video games for computers, websites, and mobile devices. You can use your knowledge of game engines, programming languages, graphics, sound, and storytelling. You can work on your own or as part of a team, and create games that entertain and challenge players

Computer science internships: You can apply for internships at various companies or organizations that offer computer science-related positions, such as software development, data analysis, cyber security, machine learning, and more. You can learn from professionals, work on real-world projects, and network with potential employers
